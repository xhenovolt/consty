Proposal Overview

DRAIS is a fully integrated school management platform designed to streamline operations, enhance communication, and improve efficiency. This proposal outlines the features, benefits, and pricing for deploying DRAIS in your institution.

Core Features

  • Student Management: Manage student profiles, admissions, enrollment, and guardians. Includes bulk imports, enrollment history, and contact management.
  • Attendance: Track attendance manually or integrate with biometric systems (fingerprint, RFID, or facial recognition). Includes daily roll call and time tracking.
  • Reports & Templates: Create customizable report templates with a Canva-like editor. Export reports as PDFs and generate print-ready layouts.
  • Finance & Fees: Automate fee collection with mobile money and payment aggregator integrations. Generate receipts and reconcile payments seamlessly.
  • Academics: Manage academic years, terms, timetables, exams, results, and report card generation. Includes metrics for student performance and rankings.
  • Users & Roles: Implement role-based access control (RBAC) for administrators, teachers, and parents. Includes audit logs and activity tracking.
